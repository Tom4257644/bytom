Suggest a Game

Got an idea you want to see built? Tell us your concept and why it’s fun or unique. No code needed — just your best pitch.

  • Describe the core loop (what does the player do in 10–30 seconds?).
  • Explain the challenge and how difficulty scales.
  • Mention any inspirations or reference links.

Submit a Game (GitHub)

Already built something? Awesome. We accept public GitHub repos with a clean, minimal structure:

  • index.html
  • styles.css
  • script.js
  • assets/ (images, sounds, etc.)
  • README.md (how to play + any credits)
  • Nothing else — no build systems or extra folders, please.
Make the repository public and share the link via the form below.
